## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no signup wall. You fill in your stack, you get the audit instantly. We ask for your email at the end — only after you've seen your results.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price in the audit engine traces to an official vendor pricing page, verified at submission time. Sources are listed in our public PRICING_DATA.md. We update them regularly.

**Q: What does the audit actually check?**
A: Three things: (1) whether you're on the right plan for your team size and usage, (2) whether two tools you're paying for do the same thing, and (3) whether a cheaper alternative fits your use case better.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: Your audit result is stored so we can generate a shareable link. Company name and email are never included in the public version — only tool names and savings figures.

**Q: What is Credex and why is it mentioned?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI credits for tools like Cursor, Claude, and ChatGPT Enterprise — sourced from companies that overforecast. For audits showing significant savings, Credex is often the fastest way to capture those savings. It's only surfaced when genuinely relevant to your results.
